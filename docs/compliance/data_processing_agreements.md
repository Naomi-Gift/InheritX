# Data Processing Agreements (DPAs)

## Overview
This document serves as a registry of our Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with third-party vendors and sub-processors. It ensures that any third party processing personal data on our behalf adheres to GDPR and our internal security standards.

## Approved Sub-Processors

| Sub-Processor | Service Provided | Location of Processing | DPA Status |
|---------------|------------------|------------------------|------------|
| AWS           | Cloud Hosting    | US-East, EU-Central    | Signed     |
| Sentry        | Error Tracking   | US                     | Signed     |
| SendGrid      | Email Delivery   | US                     | Signed     |

## DPA Requirements
All third-party vendors must agree to:
- Process data solely on our documented instructions.
- Ensure that persons authorized to process the data have committed themselves to confidentiality.
- Take all necessary technical and organizational measures to ensure data security.
- Assist us in fulfilling our obligations to respond to data subject requests.
