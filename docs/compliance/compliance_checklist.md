# Compliance Checklist

This checklist is used during quarterly audits to ensure ongoing adherence to SOC2, ISO27001, and GDPR standards.

## Security & Privacy
- [ ] Review access logs for unauthorized attempts.
- [ ] Verify that all employee access is restricted by the principle of least privilege.
- [ ] Confirm that all databases are encrypted at rest.
- [ ] Ensure TLS 1.3 is enforced on all endpoints.

## Data Processing
- [ ] Verify that DSAR requests were processed within the 30-day legal window.
- [ ] Confirm that cryptographic erasure was performed for deleted accounts.
- [ ] Review current Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with third-party vendors.

## Incident Management
- [ ] Review the incident response log for the quarter.
- [ ] Confirm that any security incidents were reported to the relevant authorities within 72 hours.
- [ ] Verify that post-mortem analyses were completed for all critical incidents.
